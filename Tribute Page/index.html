...Sushant Singh Rajput...

(1986 - 2020)

Sushant Singh Rajput (21 January 1986 – 14 June 2020) was an Indian actor known for his work in Hindi cinema.Rajput started his career after dropping out of his engineering course at the Delhi College of Engineering and entering the theatre industry in Mumbai. He moved on to feature in Hindi television serialsHe made his film debut with the film adaptation Kai Po Che! ...Some Amazing Facts...

  • 1. Sushant was a National level Olympiad winner in Physics. He had cleared as many as 11 engineering entrance exams. He also stood at 7th Rank AIEEE in 2003.
  • 2. After completing 3 years in Delhi School of Engineering, he dropped out to chase his dream of becoming an actor.
  • 3. Sushant was a part of Nadira Babbar’s Ekjute theatre group and Shiamak Davar’s dance group when he was in Delhi.
  • 4.Sushant was an ambidextrous person who could write with both hands.
  • 5. Sushant had even visited NASA to train himself for the role in space movie Chanda Mama Door Ke.
